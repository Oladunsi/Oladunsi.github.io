About Me

A little bit about me

I am a teacher turned Python Developer, who believes in individualistic reasoning. I am on a course in pursuit of a positive change in my generation. I strongly believe that i can be part of great ideas and innovations that can change my world one code at a time .

I am positive person who has quite an enthusiastic outlook on life. I love building technologies and teaching, so much that i get a great sense of achievement from seeing my students develop and grow as responsible members of their society. While that has been said, if i can have a positive impact on their future, Such that my students will not just participate as consumers, but rather designers or creators of tecnological innovations or inventions . I believe we should emphasize that our young minds develop their own knowledge and practices that is needed to create the type of dynamism and interactive computational media that they enjoy in their daily lives.

Therefor, Enegaging young minds in the creation of computational artifacts prepares young people for more than careers as computer scientists or as programmers. It supports young people’s development as computational thinkers – individuals who can draw on computational concepts, practices, and perspectives in all aspects of their lives, across disciplines and contexts.
